Welcome

Hey! I'm Hana, a.k.a Frizz Kid. I'm an illustrator and writer living in Toronto. I love tea, opossums, and Halloween.

Check out my store!

Writing & Books

My work explores themes such as feminism, body politics, racism, and pop culture. My first book, It Begins with the Body, was listed by CBC as one of the Best Poetry Books of 2018. My second book came out in September of 2020!
